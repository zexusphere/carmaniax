    Frequently Asked Questions

    How can I sell my used car via ZigWheels Philippines?

    The answer is rather an easy one, simply enter all the required information like the specifications of your car, its images, and some of your details like contact number, city, and others. Your car's ad will go live following verification. Also, please make sure to provide a valid and active contact number so that interested buyers can contact you without a fuss. In order to attract more potential buyers, kindly upload high-resolution images of both the exterior and the interior of your used car.

    Is any kind of registration required?

    There is no specific registration process that you need to go through before listing your used car for sale. Just provide us with the required information that you're asked while listing your car and an account will be automatically generated. All of the quotes and messages from the interested customers will come to you via this automatically created account.
