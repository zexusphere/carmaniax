    About Suzuki APV

    For a practical driver who wants to have versatile uses for their car, the Suzuki APV (All-Purpose Van) is worth looking at. Its large and boxy body already gives you a hint of how generous its interior space is. One of the cheapest vans in the Philippines, the Suzuki APV can accommodate up to 8 people, making it a great vehicle for families. You can also use it for delivering your business goods or as a rental vehicle.
